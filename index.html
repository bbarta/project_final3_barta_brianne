Who We Are

Welcome to Central Perk, the friendliest cafe in all of Manhattan! Our cafe is the perfect place to sit back, relax, and enjoy a cup o' joe with your closest friends. Our food and drinks are both tasty and affordable, and we offer a casual atmosphere. We even have a comfy couch! Make sure to leave your stress at the door. Don't worry, we'll always be there for you. Stop by soon, and bring a friend!
